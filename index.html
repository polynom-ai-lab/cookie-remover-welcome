Cookie Remover

Fast cleanup for cookies & site data

Welcome to Cookie Remover

You’re ready to clear cookies, cache, and local site data in a couple of clicks. Use the side panel for detailed control, or the floating banner for quick cleanup on the current site.

Open Chrome extensions How it works
1

Pin the extension

Open the Extensions menu in Chrome and pin Cookie Remover so it is always one click away.

2

Open the side panel

Click the extension icon to open the full interface with cleanup modes, storage view, cookie details, and extra controls.

3

Clean the current site

Choose whether to remove cookies, site data, or both — then run cleanup instantly.

What you can do

🧹

Quick cleanup
Use the floating banner for immediate action on the current website.

📊

See what the site stores
Track cookies and measurable local site data before and after cleanup.

🛡️

Block cookie re-add
Prevent a site from immediately setting cookies again after cleanup.
